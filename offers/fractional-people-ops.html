Who this is for

Distributed or remote-first companies, typically Series A through C, headcount between 30 and 150. The system is built or partially built. You’re hiring across timezones, running global payroll through an EOR, and adding people month over month. You need someone to run the function, not build it again from scratch. You’re not ready to hire a full-time Head of People, and you don’t need one yet. You need the work done, the documentation maintained, and the vendors handled by someone who operates without daily supervision.

How this is different from a generalist fractional

Most fractional people ops work is consultant-style: a monthly call, a few documents, occasional advice. This is execution-led. I run your operations, I don’t advise on them. The retainer has fixed deliverables, fixed scope, fixed hand-offs. If a vendor needs to be evaluated, I evaluate. If an offer letter needs to go out, I send it. If a process needs to be rewritten, I rewrite it. You sign off, I execute. The shift is from fractional advice to fractional operator.
