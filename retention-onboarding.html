What you get
  • An employee journey map from pre-boarding through the first year, marking the moments that decide whether someone stays
  • An onboarding toolkit: checklists, role-specific templates, and a day-one schedule that delivers operational readiness from the first day
  • A retention framework built as scheduled accountability, not a goodwill gesture: defined touchpoints, a check-in cadence, and an owner for each one
  • Early-warning signals for attrition, so a flight risk is visible before the resignation, not after
  • Manager talking points for the check-ins, so retention does not depend on one person remembering to care
Who this is for

You are a startup or scale-up hiring quickly and losing people inside the first year. Onboarding is different every time. There is no structured check-in after week one, so problems surface as resignations rather than as something you could have fixed. Attrition is creeping up and you are re-filling the same roles you just filled. You want new hires productive faster and staying longer, without a full-time people team to run it.
