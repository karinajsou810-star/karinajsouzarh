Internal communications diagnostic and employee enablement program

Internal Communications · Diagnostic methodology · Training design

Context

A nine-person hospitality SMB in Portugal, owner-operated, serving both restaurant and pastry. I engaged as an independent HR consultant to diagnose internal communication gaps and design an employee enablement program to address them.

Challenge

The company had strong product and a loyal local customer base, but inconsistent operational execution. Customer reviews showed recurring complaints about long wait times and inconsistent service delivery. Internally, the team operated without formalized communication protocols, no training program for service standards, and no feedback loop between customer reviews and operational changes. External brand presence was limited to social media, with no website or coordinated visual identity. The actual problem was not product quality. It was internal coordination and operational consistency.

Approach

I applied my 5-system internal communications diagnostic to map the gaps. The framework covers five operational systems: operational (communicate to function), identification (be recognized to be known), belonging (communicate to share values), audit and control (communicate to organize feedback), and commercial (communicate to sell). I gathered diagnostic data through an owner interview, an anonymous internal survey with five of nine employees responding, customer review analysis, and an online presence audit.

The diagnostic revealed that internal communication was WhatsApp-only with no formal protocols, customer feedback was not connected to operational change, and external brand presence was fragmented. I designed an external campaign that included a promotional video, a branded slogan, an outdoor design, two flyers, and an institutional website with menu, reservation system, and customer feedback form.

I created a four-module internal training program delivered through a shared Teams folder with video content for each module: introduction to communication best practices, customer service standards, order organization, and effective communication. I also built a monitoring proposal outlining metrics for tracking internal protocol adoption, customer feedback loop effectiveness, and brand consistency across channels.

Outcomes

  • Six distinct internal communication gaps identified across the 5-system framework
  • A four-module training program operational for ongoing employee enablement and skill reinforcement
  • External brand consistency established through slogan, visual identity, and dedicated website
  • A customer feedback loop created through the website form and ongoing review monitoring
  • Website live as an operational deliverable, serving as customer-facing communication infrastructure

Skills demonstrated

Diagnostic framework application, internal communications audit, customer research analysis, training program design, brand strategy, feedback system design, employee training delivery.
