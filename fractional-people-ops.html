What’s included each month
  • Onboarding handled for every new hire: the same process every time, documented, predictable, no founder involvement required

  • EOR, payroll, and benefits vendor coordination. I stay the point of contact so you don’t get pulled into operational questions

What this is not: a retainer where you pay for access and I wait for tickets. Each month has a fixed scope, fixed priorities, and a clear output. You know what you’re buying.
